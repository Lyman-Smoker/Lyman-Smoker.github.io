Yuan-Ming Li

I am currently a second-year Ph.D student at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU) advised by Prof. Wei-Shi Zheng. Before coming to SYSU, I obtained my B.E. degree at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.

My research interests mainly lie around Computer Vision, with the goal of developing general and generalizable vision systems. My main research focuses on Video Action Understanding.

In the following years, I will focus on Interpretable Action Assessment and Egocentric Action Understanding. And I am always open for research discussions and collaborations.
